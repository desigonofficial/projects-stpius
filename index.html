OUR AIM
The aim of this institution is to impart sound education to the young, so as to enable them to find their proper place in the society in their later life. We lay great emphasis on character formation and cultural behavior of children. St. Pius Convent School is concerned with the complete education, so that students become enlightened individuals to serve the society
OUR VISION
Our vision is to form persons who foster universal and eternal values and making them fully alive to bring about a just society.
OUR MISSION
Our mission is to shape the children into intellectually competent, morally sound, psychologically integrated, emotionally stable and socially committed citizens.
